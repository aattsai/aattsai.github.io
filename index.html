I met this 48yo Colombian man who was celebrating the completion of his 12-step at ca. He showed me his artwork, and I was stunned by the abstraction and how unsettling it made me feel.

He asked me to comment, I told him that it makes me feel disturbed. He slammed on the bar counter and laughed "so I am an artist after all. You see, its not about the bullshit answer like 'oh yeah its good' or trying to analyze it, you can't analyze art. Its about the reaction, you felt disturbed by my art, it moved you on the inside, I moved you, a stranger just touched your world."

When asked about his decision to not sell his artworks, he said "I tried once at a bar in Astoria 13 years ago. I asked every buyer why they wanted my painting. One couple said the color really goes with our couch. I raged and told them to get the f*** outta here, my pain and suffering goes with your couch? I returned all the proceeds to the bar owner and asked her to comp drinks that night until everyone gets wasted, then use the rest to get me breakfast." He left this self-portrait on a receipt before leaving the bar.
